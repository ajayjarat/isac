Who ISAC Serves?

Features

All Government organizations subject to NIST/FISMA security requirements involving DISA STIG/SCAP compliance or a growing set of supplemental standards. This includes all of DoD and other agencies, e.g., DHS, FBI, Veteran, etc.

HOW ISAC Does It?

Features

Compared to conventional STIG/SCAP benchmarks and processes (and other auditing and remediation products), ISAC’s hyper-automation, end-to-end lifecycle coverage, and centralized control plus collaboration and visuals are totally disruptive.

To facilitate adoption, key ISAC screens appear virtually identical to their DoD STIG Viewer counterparts.

ISAC automatically interfaces directly with all source and target DoD systems.

Leverages cutting edge technology and absolute best practices (we are inventors of successful industry methods) to maximize ISAC’s enduring value.

What is ISAC?

Features

ISAC is the disruptive cybersecurity system proven to offer 100% compliance for DISA STIG/SCAP Standards in mid-large scale government enterprises.

With nearly 200 active users and 2,000+ servers under watch, and growing, ISAC has earned major kudos from the client(s) and cybersecurity experts and is positioned for exponential growth.

What ISAC does?

Features

ISAC hyper-automates all end-to-end aspects of cybersecurity posture, including Scanning, Documentation, Remediation, Reporting & Analytics.

From a single pane of glass, ISAC starts with bulk acquisition and deployment of all STIG, SCAP, and other DoD/FISMA/NIST compliance benchmarks across hundreds or thousands of servers.

It then provides central orchestration of vulnerability assessments, follow-up, remediation (incl false positives), reminders, audit ep/closure/scoring, gap collaboration, dashboarding (incl heat maps), and more.

ISAC saves hundreds of hours per month in laborious and error prone technical security SOPs.
